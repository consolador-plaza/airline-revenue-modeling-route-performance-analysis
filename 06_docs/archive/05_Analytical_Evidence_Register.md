## Overall Assessment

The evidence collected throughout the project consistently supports the conclusion that the integrated analytical model is statistically sound, commercially meaningful, and suitable for business intelligence reporting. Independent analyses of concentration, distribution, relationships, growth, capacity utilization, and robustness reinforce one another rather than produce conflicting interpretations. This provides a strong foundation for dashboard development and executive reporting.
